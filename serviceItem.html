PHYSICAL THERAPY SERVICES in Brooklyn

What to Expect from our Physical Therapists.

Our Physical Therapists are experts in musculoskeletal system who treat patients of all ages with health-related conditions to reduce pain and improve or restore mobility. As highly trained healthcare professionals, our therapists create personal treatment plans that meet the patient’s healthcare and post-operative rehabilitation needs. We work closely with physicians to return patients to pre-injury levels. Our rehabilitation services keep patients moving forward, so they can return to day-to-day work and recreational activities and remain at their optimal health without further risk of injury.

Physical Therapy Techniques

  • Physical Therapy
  • Education about your condition
  • Stretching to reduce stress on joints and improve flexibility
  • Manual therapy to improve or maintain range of motion
  • Exercise to strengthen muscles and to improve coordination, balance, and endurance
  • Postural re-education
  • Home-exercise program instruction
  • Injury prevention
  • Modalities such as: Ultrasound, Electrical Stimulation, Traction, Cold Laser Therapy, Vibration Therapy and Kinesiotaping.
