Welcome to Back to Health Physical
& Occupational Therapy

If you are injured, in pain, or recovering from surgery, you can trust the doctors at the Back to Health Physical and Occupational Therapy to help you restore function, alleviate pain and prevent disabilities resulting from physical traumas.

Since 1995, Back to Health clinics have gained the reputation as Brooklyn's leading physical and occupational therapy facilities designed to provide comprehensive diagnostic and therapeutic services. For more than two decades, we have successfully treated complex neurological, orthopedic and musculoskeletal conditions as well as work-related and athletic injuries.

Our highly trained team of physical and occupational therapy doctors is committed to rehabilitating each patient to his or her maximum potential.

Conveniently located in the heart of Sheepshead Bay, Midwood, Bensonhurst and Flatbush, Back to Health clinics are physical therapist-owned and operated outpatient facilities featuring a full line of the latest equipment from the world's leading manufacturers.

Our entire team of medical professionals works tirelessly to deliver positive experiences to our patients. From the initial evaluation to discharge, our friendly and knowledgeable staff will help make your treatment as comfortable and stress-free as possible. To better serve our diverse patient base, our staff members are fluent in Russian, Spanish, Polish and Ukrainian.

It is through this commitment to continuous improvement that patients and referral sources can be confident they are working with New York specialists in the field of orthopedic rehabilitation, physical therapy, occupational therapy, sports medicine, and outreach services.

If you have any questions or you like to schedule a visit. Please contact our patient support department.
